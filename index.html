Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my compressed image guaranteed to be smaller?

Our smart algorithm uses multiple compression techniques and automatically selects the best format and settings to ensure your image is always smaller than the original.

What file formats are supported?

We support JPEG, PNG, WebP, GIF, and BMP formats. Our "Auto" mode intelligently selects the best format for maximum compression.

Is my data safe?

Yes! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers and metadata is removed by default.

What's the maximum file size?

Each image can be up to 10MB in size. For batch processing, you can upload multiple files simultaneously.
